A Relaxing Method for Dental Treatment

Sedation is a special relaxing method used during dental treatment to allow the patient to undergo the procedure without pain and stress. This method reduces patient discomfort and fear, providing them with a calm and relaxed state.

Benefits of Sedation

Who is it for?

Children afraid of the dentist

An ideal method to reduce children's discomfort and fear during treatment.

Sensitive and nervous patients

Minimizes stress, making treatment comfortable and safe.

Long and complex treatments

Ensures patient comfort during treatment and improves cooperation.

Surgical procedures

Reduces pain and stress, ensuring safer procedures.

Thanks to sedation, treatment becomes painless, stress-free, and safer. During the procedure, the patient relaxes, their ability to cooperate increases, and the effect of sedation gradually disappears after treatment. Thus, both physical and psychological comfort are ensured, making the treatment more pleasant.
